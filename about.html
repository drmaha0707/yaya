What do you know about YAYA?

YayaTraveler was established as one of the leading tour operators for Sri Lanka long before the island became one of the hottest holiday destinations in the world. Founded in 2015, YayaTraveler has quietly built a reputation for possessing an intimate, in-depth knowledge of Sri Lanka. We are fervently independent and our advice on what’s hot and what’s not remains up-to-the-minute and religiously followed by a loyal and growing clientele.

Travel consultants in Sri Lanka

Our travel consultants in Sri Lanka will assist you at anytime regarding your tours with us.

Experience

We have been organizing tours more than 10 years and the experienced gathered from previous tours are always used as inputs to make your tour even better.

Personal Attention

Every tour is given our complete personal attention irrespective of the number of guests.

Flexibility

You have complete control of your tour and you are free to organize it in any way you like. We will help you with our expert advice so that your tour is expedited smoothly.

Professional Staff

All inquiries and tours will be handled by professionally qualified staff who have many years of experience in the tourism industry.

We live it, breathe it and most of our best ideas and innovations have evolved from our first-hand experiences on the road.
